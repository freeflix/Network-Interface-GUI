YouTube subscriber counter

The subscriber count displayed on YouTube is often incorrect because it doesn't update in realtime. That's why we developed the YouTube Subscriber Counter.

The YouTube live counter is the best way to stay up to date with YouTube achievements because the data is realtime. Besides the live YouTube subscriber count, you can also get statistics like the subscriber count that YouTube shows, the total amount of video views and the total amount of videos. Besides that we show the latest video's of the YouTuber of your choise in our videofeed and reward everyone who tweets about us with a spot in the twitterfeed. Our data comes straight from the YouTube API which makes it 100% accurate. You will no longer miss any milestones!

How does it work?

  1. Enter your YouTube name, username or channel ID and press search (or enter).
  2. The counter gets your live data
  3. Feel free to share your data on Twitter or Facebook!
